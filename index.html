Top MTurk Tips for Maximizing Your Earnings

  • Find High Paying HITs: Look for HITs that pay well per hour. Focus on tasks that align with your skills and interests.
  • Take Qualification Tests: Boost your eligibility for higher-paying tasks by completing qualification tests offered by requesters.
  • Work Efficiently: Use browser extensions and scripts to automate repetitive tasks and save time.
  • Check Requester Reviews: Use Turkerview to review the reputation of requesters to avoid poor-paying tasks and maximize your earnings.
  • Complete HITs Quickly: The faster you complete tasks, the more you can earn in a given time. But don't sacrifice quality for speed.

Essential Tools for MTurk Workers

To increase your efficiency and maximize your earnings, consider using these tools:

  • Turkopticon: A browser extension that helps you evaluate requesters' reputation based on worker reviews.
  • MTurk Suite: A suite of tools for filtering tasks, tracking earnings, and automating workflows.
  • AutoHotKey Scripts: Automate repetitive tasks like copying and pasting responses using AutoHotKey scripts.
  • Worker Insights: Provides data on tasks, pay rates, and time requirements to help you select the best HITs.
